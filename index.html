Our Services

RCC Breaker

Our RCC breaker service specializes in breaking reinforced concrete structures with precision and safety. We use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure minimal vibration and dust, making the process clean and efficient.

RCC Wall Cutting

Precision wall cutting services for creating openings in concrete walls without compromising structural integrity. Ideal for doorways, windows, or expansion projects with clean, straight cuts every time.

Core Cutting

Professional core cutting for creating precise circular openings in concrete slabs, walls, and floors. Perfect for HVAC installations, plumbing, electrical conduits, and other utility passages.

Rebaring & Anchor Bolt Fixing

Expert reinforcement services including rebar installation and anchor bolt fixing to ensure structural strength and stability. We follow strict engineering specifications for all reinforcement work.

Slab Cutting

Precision slab cutting services for partial or complete removal of concrete slabs. We handle everything from small residential projects to large commercial slab removals with minimal disruption.
